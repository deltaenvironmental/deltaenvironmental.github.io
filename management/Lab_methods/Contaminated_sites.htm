CONTAMINATED SITE ASSESSMENT EQUIPMENT LIST

Updated: 26 February 2003 (date added)

Safety:

  • First aid kit
  • Fire extinguisher
  • PPE - Safety helmets, steel capped boots, safety glasses, ear plugs, disposable gloves, disposable overalls
  • Traffic control gear and signage

Sampling equipment:

  • Sampling schedule/log
  • Stainless steel spade
  • Stainless steel pan
  • Hand auger
  • Pick/crowbar
  • Containers for samples - prepared sample bottles 1 prepared trip blank
  • Waterproof marking pens for identification of samples
  • Insulated carrier boxes
  • Ice

Decontamination equipment:

  • Ground sheet
  • 3 wash buckets
  • 20L container deionised water
  • Phosphate-free detergent
  • Paper towels
  • Plastic scrubbing brush
  • Garbage bags to hold disposed paper towels, gloves, and overalls

Recording equipment:

  • Chain of custody forms
  • Analysis request forms
  • Waterproof ink pens
  • Notebook
  • Global Positioning System - handheld (or tape measures - at least 20m)
  • Camera
  • Film and batteries
