Legislation, National/ International Standards, Codes of Practice, Guidelines

Overview: National/ International Standards, Codes of Practice and Legislation are maintained by the Owner, who ensures that appropriate hardcopy documents are available within the Company, and disposes of earlier versions as new documentation becomes available. External suppliers of documentation are contacted regularly to ascertain that the documents held remain current. Delta encourages all personnel to check for the latest on-line versions of these types of documents to avoid the possibility of using obsolete documents.
