Delta Environmental Consulting

Job description – Technician or assistant (T)

11 November 2008 (addition of premises support)

Technicians, assistants and trainees work under supervision. Some undertake work in a specific area of the business (laboratory, administration, personnel & premises, or field work) while others are generalists who undertake some duties across all these fields.

Administrative support

  • Answering the phone
  • Filing
  • Monthly accounts, GST and BAS
  • Purchasing stationery supplies
  • Invoicing customers for work completed
  • Build good relationships with clients
  • Organising travel and accomodation for fieldwork
  • Attending meetings with senior consultant to review work progress

Fieldwork support

  • Preparing equipment for fieldwork
  • Assisting with fieldwork using established methods
  • Packing and cleaning up after fieldwork
  • Preparation of specimens, and preparation and transport of soil/water samples
  • Flora and fauna identification
  • Locate geographic features using a GPS

Project desk work support

  • Land title and usage history searches
  • Aerial photography history searches
  • Aerial photograph visual analysis
  • Data entry of field data (geographic and biological data)
  • Compile species lists and compare to regional, State and national lists of significant species
  • Assist with GIS analysis of survey sites
  • Assist with mathematical modelling where required
  • Assist with ecological community analysis

Laboratory support

  • Chemical and physical soil and water testing
  • Microbiological testing
  • Plankton counts and identification
  • Quality assurance laboratory testing for external clients
  • Maintaining test equipment (including calibration and standards)

Personnel & premises support

  • Undertake care duties as required
  • Organise contractors for maintenance of premises
  • Undertake day to day cleaning and routine maintenance of premises

Purchasing support

  • Order supplies as required, from approved suppliers
  • Purchase consumable requirements on a regular basis
  • Control of supplies
  • Protection and preservation of supplies
  • Receiving inspection

Management system responibilities in relation to the area of work

  • Undertake duties using operating procedures in the Management System
  • Provide suggestions for improving instructions and procedures
