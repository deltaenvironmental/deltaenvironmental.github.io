Lab reports - method codes

Updated: 15 May 2013 (new reference standards added)

Return to Management Manual

Lab reports contain space to detail the methodology used to make the determinations. Details of Delta's commonly used test methods and information about speciation of analyses are provided here.

The minimum requirement on lab reports is to report the method used, as detailed in the Method column below. Additional details from the Reference and Special notes columns may be incorporated as necessary.

In some cases several methods are in common use. In such a case, select the mthod used to conduct the specific analysis being reported.
